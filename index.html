Normal Order λ Calculus Evaluator.

Insert some λ calculus into the text area and press go to evaluate it. Variable names can only consist of one letter. The expression ab will represent the function a applied to b. Typing a backslash (\) results in the lambda symbol. If you want to declare variables, add statements in the beginning and end them with ;. After the last ; you should put the expression you want to evaluate. Any symbols other than λ . ( ) ; and whitespace will be treated as variables.
